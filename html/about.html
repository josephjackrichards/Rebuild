Just getting started? Beginner

With the Beginner level, you will receive one Beginner level puzzle a month. You will be placed into Beginner level communities, where you will work together to complete your puzzles. These puzzles range from 100-500 piece jigsaw puzzles. The Beginner Level service is $10 bucks a month.

Ready for an upgrade? Intermediate Level

With the Intermediate Level, you will receive one Intermediate level puzzle a month. You will be placed into Intermediate level communities, where you will work together to complete your puzzles or race against each other to see who can complete their puzzles the fastest. Fastest completions win cash prizes. Intermediate puzzels range from 100-1000 piece jigsaw puzzles. The Intermediate level service is $15 bucks a month.

Feeling Lucky? Expert Level

With the Expert Level, you will receive unlimited puzzles a month. Once you complete a puzzle and send it back, we will send you a new one. These puzzles range from 100-5000 piece puzzles. They can be regular jigsaw, wooden, 3D and everything inbetween. You will be placed in Expert Level communities where you will work together to complete puzzles or race against each other for the honor of REBUILD CHAMPION. Do you have what it takes? The Expert Level service is $30 bucks a month.
