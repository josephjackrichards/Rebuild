About Rebuild

As my wife and I were walking along the beach in Wilea Hawaii, we were thinking about how fractured our communities were and how distant we felt with our families. After being trapped inside for almost 18 months, we had taken up the hobby of building puzzles and LOVED it. We always wanted to share our puzzles with others, but just didnt know how to do it. That's how Rebuild was born. One piece at a time, we worked and brainstormed until we got what we have today!

We guarantee that you will develop a love for puzzles and want to bring everyone you know into your communities one piece at a time. Thank you for being a part of our ever growing family and trying to make the world a more connected place.
